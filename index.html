Tones

I have a small guitar collection going, and I love every guitar I own. As I've slowly leveled up as a guitarist, I've started to appreciate the importance of tone. Since I can't easily play my guitars side-by-side to compare their sound, I recorded them all and put the recordings on this page for quick, easy review.

What considerations did you take?

I recorded each guitar both clean and distorted. For both clean and distorted recordings, I also provided a sample for both bridge and neck pickups. So, each guitar has four total recordings available for review here. I did not change any amp settings for this project, because I wanted as much as possible to provide an apples-to-apples comparison. I ran the guitars through my Yamaha THX10II amp, with center EQ settings, high gain, and no effects other than a smidge of reverb. The only adjustments I made were:

  • Recording volume, to balance clarity vs clipping
  • Tone knob on the guitar (for clean only) to give each guitar a shot to put its best foot forward
